My SSTC with inspiration from the designs of Labcoatz.

I've always wanted an archade, so why not make one? Very fun project.

To prep for working with Scanning Probe Microscopes I built a robot that goes against a wall and plots out the topology, roughly similar to how a SPM works.

My LED Tetris board was a fun project I did for a final at Harvard Summer School. The YouTube channel "ELECTRONOOBS" provided a great guide about doing something similar.

The haptic pen we were able to connect to the SPM, along with the surface topography (red dot signifies pen position).
